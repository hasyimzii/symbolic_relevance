The value of exfoliative cytology of the urine in the diagnosis of bladder tumors.
It is reported on the cytological and histological results of 253 bladder tumors. There was an exact correspondence of the results in 80.3%; in 11.4% it was not possible to prove the existence of the bladder tumor cytologically. Most of these were low grade malignant tumors. In 8.3% of the cases the results were obtained through PAP III according to the scheme of Papanicolaou.
