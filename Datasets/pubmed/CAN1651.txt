Haemangiopericytoma of bone. Review of the literature and report of a case.
A case of intraosseous haemangiopericytoma of the humerus is described. Exarticulation at the shoulder joint was performed and the patient has been followed for 8 years, so far without developing a local recurrence or metastases. The 16 cases of intraosseous haemangiopericytomas on record are reviewed.
