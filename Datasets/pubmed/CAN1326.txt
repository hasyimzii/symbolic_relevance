Radiological appearances in early oesophageal carcinoma.
The radiological appearances in 30 cases of early oesophageal carcinoma are described. The main changes are: (1) localized superficial filling defect; (2) changes in the mucosal folds, such as thickening, tortuosity, interruption, destruction and convergence; (3) minute ulceration. Spasm and stiffness in the affected segment are frequently observed.
