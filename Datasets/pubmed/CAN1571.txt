An unusual pattern of papillary cystadenolymphoma (Warthin's tumor) of the parotid gland. Case report (author's transl).
The authors present a case of Warthin's tumor growing during 15 years and having  a very unusual hyperplastic and pseudopapillary epithelial covering. This case is studied under the already known histogenetical hypothesis which were reviewed and examined through the recent publications and the author's works and experience.
